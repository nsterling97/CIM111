Penne alla Vodka

made with

The Steinbach Family Vodka Sauce

Level: Easy
Yield: 8-10 servings

Prep time: 5 min
Cook time: 45 min

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta (preferably penne or rigatoni)
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 tsp. minced garlic
  • 6 slices prociutto
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  • 8 oz. heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup vodka
  • 1 28 oz. can crushed tomatoes

Directions

(hover over each step for a visual)
  1. Boil pasta in 4 quarts of salted water for 8-10 minutes.
  2. In a separate pot, saut√© the garlic and onion in butter.
  3. Add prociutto and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Add tomatoes and vodka, simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. Add heavy cream and parmesan cheese. Simmer until heated through.
  6. Serve sauce over cooked pasta.

Tips

  • For a healthier version use a butter substitute and whole wheat pasta.
  • Use fresh ingredients!
  • Make with love :)

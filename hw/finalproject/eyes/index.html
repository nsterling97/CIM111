The eyes are one of the most important features of the face. Use the slider above for recommended products and Youtube tutorials! Follow the tips below for the best tricks on applying eyeshadow for beginners:

  • Always start with eye primer. It helps create a smooth surface for eye makeup.
  • Start with four basic shades - a highlighter, mid-tone, contour and black shade. Find a palette made for your eye color.
  • Use the right brushes. Start with a flat brush and blending brush as essentials.
  • Apply the lightest shade in the corner, the mid-tone shade on the lid, the contour color in the crease and black shadow as a liner.
  • Eyeliner (pencil or liquid) can also be used in place of eyeshadow.
  • Get familiar with different mascara brands to see what works best for you.
  • Use an eyelash curling tool for extra length!

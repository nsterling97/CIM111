Foundation is the final touch for a flawless look. Use the slider above for recommended products and Youtube tutorials! Follow these tips for the perfect application:

  • Lighting is key when it comes to foundation. If you don't have great lighting in your room, try using a mirror with a light.
  • If you're not sure what your shade is, ask for samples at the store.
  • Find your match by testing it on your chest or your jawline, not your arm or neck.
  • Before you pick a shade, get to know your skin's undertone.
  • Start out by dabbing, not rubbing in the foundation.
  • If you prefer a brush over a sponge, choose a kabuki or a buffing brush over a paddle brush for easier blending.
  • If you're using a blending sponge, make sure you clean it properly.

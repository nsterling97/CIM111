Eyebrows compliment the eyes and are often ignored, but with a little help they make a big difference. Use the slider above for recommended products and Youtube tutorials! The following tips will give you the best guide on how to take care of those brows:

  • As a general guide, the head (A) of your eyebrow should line up with the outer edge of your nostril and the tail (C) should line up diagonally with that nostril.
  • If you want to go further, you can sculpt your eyebrows to match your face shape.
  • Invest in some basic equipment like tweezers, a brow pencil, and a spoolie brush.
  • When using the spoolie, brush the hair up and away from the nose to blend in the color.
  • While highlighter, concealer, and brow wax aren't necessary, they can really enhance your eyebrow shape and color.
  • Concealer will even out the tops and bottoms of your eyebrows and can help fix any errors.

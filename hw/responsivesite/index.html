About

Mila’s Summer Sweets is all about the best. I am always trying new things to find the best, organic and fresh, ingredients for my cakes. All of my cakes are 100% Organic because I care deeply about the animalsthat give me my raw ingredients and the ones that live in the jungles where my coffee and cocoa beans grow. Bespoke 100% Organic Cakes & Pastries. Low Sugar & Animal Friendly, with Gluten Free options.
